Dataset

The evaluation uses three datasets:

1. DroidBench – benchmark for Android taint analysis
Download:https://github.com/secure-software-engineering/DroidBench

2. UBCBench – extended privacy benchmark
Download: https://github.com/LinaQiu/UBCBench

3. 300 Privacy-Related Real Apps collected from Google Play
Download: https://drive.google.com/file/d/1WyQLIwztk5jqD0uharrhfg1Z6QUp6SJp/view?usp=sharing
